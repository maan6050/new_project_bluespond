# Bluespond -- Screen Outlines & Wireframe Descriptions

**Project:** Bluespond
**Client:** Darell Parker
**Prepared by:** Mandeep Singh
**Phase:** 1 -- Discovery & Architecture

**Note:** These are detailed screen descriptions for creating visual wireframes. The UX direction per Darell'S brief: clean, modern, user-friendly -- "more like using an iPhone or Amazon than traditional cumbersome business software." Blue-led palette: deep blue, bright blue/teal, sky blue accents, white/off-white backgrounds, soft gray, charcoal text.
